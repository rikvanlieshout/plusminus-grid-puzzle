Plus-minus grid puzzle

Puzzle ID: 1 Score: 0 Highscore: 0
  • Start on any square by selecting it.
  • You add up numbers as you move through the grid, but the sign (+/-) changes every move.
  • The game ends once you get stuck somewhere (in a corner, or behind squares you already emptied).
  • Try to find a route that maximizes your score!
